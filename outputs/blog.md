## What is the Document Compare Feature?

Genie AI’s Document Compare feature is a native tool designed to streamline your legal document review process. With this feature, you can easily identify changes between different versions of your documents, eliminating the need for manual scrolling and comparison. This intuitive tool is engineered specifically for non-lawyers, making complex legal reviews accessible and efficient.

The Document Compare feature integrates seamlessly into Genie’s real-time editor, allowing you to compare Genie documents with external files. This means you can quickly share clear, unified redlines with your team, enhancing collaboration and decision-making.

## How It Works

Using the Document Compare feature is straightforward. Here’s how you can get started:

1. **Open the Document:** Access the document you wish to compare in Genie AI’s real-time editor.
2. **Select the Version:** Choose the version you want to compare it against, whether it’s another Genie document or an external file.
3. **View Changes:** The tool will display instant visibility of changes, highlighting additions, deletions, and modifications.
4. **Review Side-by-Side:** Utilize both side-by-side and unified diff views to easily understand the differences.
5. **Export for Sharing:** Once you’ve reviewed the changes, export the results for easy sharing with your stakeholders.
